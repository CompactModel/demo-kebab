Our Story

Real Döner,
Real Tradition

Sultan Döner was founded in 2001 by Mehmet Yilmaz, who brought his family's recipe from Gaziantep to the heart of Berlin-Mitte. Every skewer is seasoned overnight, every bread is baked fresh each morning.

We use only halal-certified meat and never freeze our produce. Over 20 years, we've become a fixture of the neighborhood — a place where the line is always worth it.
